About Technology R Us

Small shop. Big help. Friendly neighborhood tech support you can trust.

Our Mission

At Technology R Us, our mission is simple: to provide our community with friendly, trustworthy, and high-quality tech services. Whether it’s repairing a broken laptop, upgrading a gaming rig, or helping you get started with a Raspberry Pi project, we treat each customer like a neighbor.

Who We Are

We’re a small team of tech enthusiasts and students who love helping people with technology. Unlike big box stores, we focus on personal service, honesty, and transparency.

Our Values

  • Friendly and approachable service
  • Transparent pricing and clear communication
  • Community-focused support
  • Continuous learning and tech expertise
